Our People

Meet the leadership team behind Saifee Quantum, driving global opportunities for Canadian businesses.

Ebrahimm Bootwala

Founder & CEO

Ebrahimm Bootwala, Founder & CEO

Ebrahimm leads Saifee Quantum with a background that spans operations, systems engineering, and financial analysis across India and the US. He served as a Director of Operations at Saifee Industries, where he leads system implementation, streamlines operations, and applies AI/ML to process data, bringing Silicon Valley operational practices to a business with over 30 years of coating and plating expertise, including nickel plating, electropolishing, anodizing, and PVD coatings through its partnership with the Hylite Group (Poona PVD LLP). Ebrahimm spent four years as a Business Intelligence Analyst at Edelman Financial Engines in Santa Clara, California, and holds an MS in Industrial & Systems Engineering from San Jose State University. Ebrahimm founded Saifee Quantum in Toronto to bring that same combination: hands-on manufacturing and coatings expertise, systems and process discipline, and financial analysis experience, to Canadian businesses looking to grow internationally.

Abhijeit Karmaker

Chief Research and Development Officer

Abhijeit Karmaker, Chief Research and Development Officer

Serves as Chief Research and Development Officer at Saifee Quantum, leading the company's technology infrastructure, market research, and product development functions. He brings a rigorous, systems-driven approach to building Saifee Quantum's export-import operations from the ground up, leading research into target markets and trade partnerships while architecting the company's internal tools and digital infrastructure with full-stack development expertise spanning cloud platforms. Based in Vancouver, BC, Abhijeit combines technical rigor with hands-on experience standing up operations for growing businesses.

Shane Smith

Senior Strategic Advisor – International Business & Global Partnerships

Shane Smith

Shane Smith serves as Senior Strategic Advisor to Saifee Quantum Inc., providing strategic guidance on international business development, global partnerships, and organizational growth. With extensive experience working alongside businesses, governments, nonprofit organizations, and international stakeholders, Shane brings valuable expertise in stakeholder engagement, partnership development, organizational strategy, and sustainable growth. His international perspective supports Saifee Quantum's mission of building meaningful relationships and creating long-term business opportunities across global markets. Working closely with the leadership team, Shane provides strategic insight that strengthens the company's international network and supports market expansion initiatives.
