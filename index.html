Backed by Saifee Industries

Built on global trade experience, made for Canadian exporters

Saifee Quantum works alongside Saifee Industries, a Pune-based manufacturing operation with over 30 years of experience in metal finishing and coating technologies, including nickel chrome plating, electroless nickel plating, copper nickel plating, stainless steel electropolishing and passivation, anodizing, and PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coatings through its Poona PVD LLP partnership with the Hylite Group.

Where other Toronto trade consultants advise from the outside, Saifee Quantum has a direct line to a working production facility, giving us a genuine head start when we begin exporting our own products.

Saifee Industries was founded in Pune in 1993 by Shabbir Bootwala, building on the electroplating foundation laid by the late Moiz Bootwala's Burhani Industries in 1979. Over three decades, the company grew under CEO Aziz Bootwala to include electropolishing, passivation, anodizing, and most recently PVD coatings.

Saifee Quantum Inc. was founded in Toronto to put that manufacturing heritage to work for Canadian businesses, helping you find customers, navigate new markets, and grow your international footprint, backed by a real production network rather than just advisory experience.
